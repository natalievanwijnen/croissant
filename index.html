Croissant-Nation

Here butter reigns supreme


Born from the alpine slopes of Austria, our journey began with the humble 'kipferl' pastry, a delightful precursor to the flaky sensation we've become. But it was the French touch that elevated us to legendary status. With Marie Antoinette as our herald, we graced the palates of the late 18th century and never looked back. Through the skilled hands of French bakers, we underwent a metamorphosis, our dough lovingly laminated with butter to create the ethereal, buttery layers that define our nation today.
